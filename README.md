## 📌 Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes the relationship between **Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption** and **Renewable Energy production** across U.S. states.

The project explores EV registration trends, renewable energy generation, and their correlation to support data-driven insights for sustainable transportation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze state-wise EV adoption
- Compare Renewable vs Fossil Energy production
- Identify top-performing states
- Explore the relationship between Renewable Energy and EV growth
- Generate actionable insights and recommendations

---

- Power BI

- Excel / CSV
- Data Visualization

## 📈 Key Insights

✅ California leads EV registrations

✅ Renewable-rich states generally show higher EV adoption

✅ Wind & Solar are major renewable energy contributors

✅ Clean energy infrastructure supports EV growth

---

## 🚀 Outcome

The analysis suggests a positive relationship between renewable energy development and electric vehicle adoption across many U.S. states.
